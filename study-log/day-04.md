## 🧠 Concepts

### Ethernet Frame Structure

Main fields:

- Destination MAC  
- Source MAC  
- Type  
- Data  
- FCS  

---

### Important Fields

**Preamble**
- Synchronizes sender and receiver  

**SFD (Start Frame Delimiter)**
- Indicates start of frame  

**Type Field**
- Identifies Layer 3 protocol  

**FCS (Frame Check Sequence)**
- Error detection  

---

### MAC Address

- Unique identifier of device  
- Works at Layer 2  

---

### Types of Communication

**Unicast**
- One to one  

**Broadcast**
- One to all  
- Example: FF:FF:FF:FF:FF:FF  

**Multicast**
- One to group
